Privacy Policy

Nest Food Delivery ("we", "our", or "us") operates the following mobile applications:

  • Nest Food Delivery – Parent App
  • Nest Food Delivery – Cuisiners App
  • Nest Food Delivery – Rider App

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our applications and services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Location data (for delivery tracking)
  • Device information
  • Order and delivery details

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process food delivery requests
  • Connect parents, riders, and cuisiners
  • Provide real-time delivery tracking
  • Improve service quality and app performance

Location Information

Our applications may collect location data to enable delivery tracking, route navigation for riders, and accurate order fulfillment. Location information is only used for service functionality and is not sold to third parties.

Data Security

Nest Food Delivery takes appropriate security measures to protect user information. All sensitive data transmitted between the mobile applications and our servers is encrypted using secure HTTPS connections during transmission.

Access to user accounts is protected through authentication mechanisms, and only authorized services are permitted to process user data. We continuously review and improve our security practices to help prevent unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure of personal information.

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell user data. Your information may only be shared with:

  • Delivery riders to complete orders
  • Cuisiners or restaurants fulfilling the order
  • Service providers necessary to operate the platform

User Account Deletion

Users can request deletion of their account and associated data at any time.

To request deletion, please visit:

https://nestfooddelivery.org/delete-account

Children’s Privacy

Nest Food Delivery applications are not intended for independent use by children under the age of 15. Our platform includes features such as food ordering, delivery coordination, and payment transactions, which require responsible supervision.

For this reason, we recommend that accounts be created and managed only by parents, guardians, or responsible adults.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without the consent and supervision of a parent or guardian. If we become aware that personal information has been collected from a child without proper authorization, we will take steps to remove such information from our systems.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
