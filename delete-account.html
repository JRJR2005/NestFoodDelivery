Delete Account

Nest Food Delivery respects your right to control your personal information. Users can request deletion of their account and associated data at any time.

How to Delete Your Account

You can request account deletion using one of the following methods:

  • Inside the mobile application (Settings → Delete Account)
  • Email request to: nestfooddelivery@gmail.com
  • Contact our support number: +91 91591 58227

After receiving the request, our team will verify the account ownership and begin the deletion process.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following information will be permanently removed:

  • User profile information (name and phone number)
  • Saved addresses
  • Order history
  • Delivery tracking records
  • Associated login credentials

Data That May Be Retained

Some limited information may be retained if required for legal, security, or financial record keeping purposes. For example:

  • Payment transaction records
  • Fraud prevention or dispute records
  • Legal compliance requirements

Any retained data will only be stored for the minimum period required by applicable laws and will not be used for marketing purposes.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are typically processed within 3–7 business days. Once the deletion process is complete, the account cannot be restored.
